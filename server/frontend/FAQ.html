FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How to play?
The word hunt game is played by building words of three or more characters.
1) First click a letter.
2) Drag your mouse over to the next letter in the word you want to form. It can be formed vertically, horizontally or diagonally,
but each successive letter must be a bordering letter.
3) When you build a word of 3+ length that is valid the word will turn green. To end the word, click again on the final letter.
4) If you attempt to trace the same word again it will appear gray in the board.
5) Attempt to build as many words as you can.
How do points work?
Points are assigned according to the length of the word.

3 letters: 100 points
4 letters: 400 points
5 letters: 800 points
6 letters: 1400 points

Each additional letter in a word is 400 additional points
Is there only one board?
Everyday an English and German board is generated. For that date those two boards are the only available boards and will be changed the next day. So play today, before they're gone!
How does the Leaderboard work?
Each board has its own leaderboard. There are two dropdown menus: date and language. To view the leaderboard select the date and language of your desired board.
For example if I wanted to see the English board's leaderboard for 12/13 I would select 12/13 and English.
How do I check my score?
Scores can be viewed in the leaderboard. Simply locate your username and you will be able to see your top score along with your longest word and word count.
What happens if I play the same board twice?
You may play the same board as many times as you want! However, only your top score will be saved to the leaderboard, along with your other stats.
